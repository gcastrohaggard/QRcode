June 2025 Topic: Heat Illness Prevention

  • Know the signs: dizziness, nausea, excessive sweating, or confusion.
  • Encourage regular hydration and access to cool areas.
  • Ensure clients and staff take breaks when working in hot spaces.
  • Report any symptoms or concerns to Safety Support immediately.

Quick Quiz

Question: What is the first thing you should do if someone appears to be suffering from heat exhaustion?

Answer: Move them to a cooler area and provide water while contacting Safety Support.
